Overlord Movie Website

A promotional movie website for the film Overlord that came out in 2018. The film was a action thriller with horror elements. For the design, I went with a darker theme to match the tone of the movie and used multiple scenes for background material.

Custom Alphabet

These are custom letters I designed for a type book. The type book revolved around positive and negative which is why the leter alternate between having black or blue as the main color. The "positive" words/letter have a more solid design compared to the "negative" words/letters which have a broken/unstable appearence.
